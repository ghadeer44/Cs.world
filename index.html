Everything About Computer Science

Computer Science is the study of the use of computers, and it contains software, hardware, internet and networking. There are many branches for the study of computers such as: software engineering, computer graphics, artificial intelligence and programming. The need of computer science as a course has been grown, because the computers become more incorporated into our daily life and technology continue to advance and develop .
